The West Vancouver Cricket Club, based at Hugo Ray Park in West Vancouver is one of the largest cricket clubs in Canada. It is rated the best cricketing facility in Western Canada.It is our aim to develop a strong and successful club both on and off the field and to provide an enjoyable environment for all members, guests and spectators. Our club field will field seven (7) competitive league teams playing in the British Columbia Mainland Cricket League (BCMCL) for the 2016 Season. The top teams play One Day International (ODI) while the rest of the teams play limited 45 overs. Games are played on Saturdays and Sundays. The regular season starts on the last weekend of April and runs to the Labour Day weekend. The club also participates in a Pre-Season Twenty20 Cricket Tournament which runs for 5 weekends prior to the start of the regular season.


Other cricket events/tournaments include:


  • Social Over 40's Cricket (mid week)
  • Junior Cricket - Under 12's and Under 17's programs
  • High peformance cricket programs
  • Summer Cricket Camp (July 4-8, 2016) at Hugo Ray Park
  • Women's Cricket (May to August)
  • The club supports and provides coaches for School Cricket
  • Social games during the season
  • Mid-week Twenty20 Cricket
  • 6-A-Side Tournaments
  • Matches against touring teams
  • Community and social cricket on Fridays from 6-8 pm (June - August)

Our club's home, Hugo Ray Park, has great facilities including 2 outdoor nets and 2 grounds - changing rooms with showers and ample free parking. The club is fortunate to have a clubhouse for use by members and guests. The clubhouse is located adjacent to the two cricket grounds and has numerous amenities including a licensed bar, a pool table and a ping pong table, kitchen, BBQ, and lounge and bar areas leading onto balconies which overlook the grounds. The clubhouse is also available for rental.

FACILITIES

Located in West Vancouver with views of the scenic North Shore mountains, Hugo Ray Park is home to the West Vancouver Cricket Club and their clubhouse. The fields are used primarily for cricket in the summer with soccer being played in the winter months.
The Pavilion is available for public rentals. Groups can host seminars and it is the perfect alternative for corporate meetings. Our downstairs board room can accommodate up to 25 people for a meeting or training session.
The upstairs bar and lounge is ideal for special occasions such as wedding receptions, birthday parties, anniversaries, cocktail receptions and various other private and promotional events. The seating capacity is 70 for a formal lunch or dinner and 90 for a reception style event. There is also a separate dance floor with facilities for a band or other similar entertainment. The lounge boasts expansive windows with views of the grounds and mountains.
The doors open up onto two large adjoining balconies with access to the grounds providing space for an extension to your indoor area on beautiful summer days! Ample free parking is available.
The Clubhouse has a small kitchen with a stove and two refrigerators that can be used by caterers, depending on the event. We do not provide catering services but guests can hire their own caterer for their specific needs.

If you are interested in renting the facility, please contact us for further details and we will be happy to assist you with your arrangements. The clubhouse is easily accessible from Highway 1 and Taylor Way.
