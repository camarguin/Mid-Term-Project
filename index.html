WEST VANCOUVER CRICKET CLUB CODE OF CONDUCT FOR MEMBERS Members are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that will enhance the reputation of West Vancouver Cricket Club and of Cricket in general, both on and off the field. Players are expected to abide by the provisions of the Code of Conduct of the British Columbia Mainland Cricket League. The Club expects members to aid in the organization of and to participate in Club events and functions. Annual subscriptions must be paid on or before the first game of the year, for current members or upon acceptance of membership, for new members. Payment of subs will be a criterion in the team selection process. NO PAY, NO PLAY. The Selection Committee will carry out team selections.

ABOUT

The West Vancouver Cricket Club, based at Hugo Ray Park in West Vancouver is one of the largest cricket clubs in Canada. It is rated the best cricketing facility in Western Canada. It is our aim to develop a strong and successful club both on and off the field and to provide an enjoyable environment for all members, guests and spectators.
